Euler Bernoulli Beam Theory

Possibly the simplest beam theory.

Kinematical Assumptions

  • Mid plane sees no strain.
  • Plane section perpendicular to the mid line before bending remain perpendicular after bending.
  • All strains and rotations are small.

When \(a \ne 0\), there are two solutions to \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) and they are \[x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a}.\]
